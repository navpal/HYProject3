How To Play

  1. The computer will present you with a scrambled word

  2. Your goal is to figure out which word got scrambled

  3. Once you have made your decision, enter it for review

  4. If you were correct, you will be presented with another word and awared a point. If you were incorrect, you can try again or skip this word for the price of one point

  5. After your time is up, share your high score with your friends and/or enemies on social media platforms like twitter
